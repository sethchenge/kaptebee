Meet Our Leadership

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Principal

Dr. Johnson brings over 20 years of experience in STEM education leadership. With a Ph.D. in Educational Leadership and a background in Chemistry, she has transformed our academy into a center of excellence for innovative learning. Her vision emphasizes project-based learning, technology integration, and collaborative partnerships with industry leaders.

Contact

Prof. Michael Chen

Deputy Principal

Prof. Chen oversees curriculum development and student affairs at STEM Academy. With a Master's in Education Technology and extensive experience in Mathematics education, he has implemented innovative assessment methods and personalized learning programs that have significantly improved student outcomes and engagement.

Contact

Dr. Emily Nguyen

Head of Science Department

Dr. Nguyen leads our Science Department with expertise in Biology and Environmental Science. Her research background in genetic engineering brings real-world applications into the classroom. She has established partnerships with local research institutions, providing students with internship opportunities and access to cutting-edge laboratory equipment.

Contact

Mr. James Rodriguez

Head of Technology Department

Mr. Rodriguez brings industry experience from Silicon Valley to our Technology Department. His specialization in computer science and artificial intelligence has revolutionized our coding and robotics programs. Under his guidance, our students consistently excel in national technology competitions and hackathons.

Contact

Dr. Robert Patel

Head of Mathematics Department

Dr. Patel combines theoretical mathematics with practical applications in our curriculum. His innovative approach includes computational thinking and data science integration. He has developed a progressive mathematics framework that prepares students for advanced studies while making complex concepts accessible through visual learning and real-world problem-solving.
